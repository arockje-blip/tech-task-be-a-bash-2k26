# 🌶️ Tamil Nadu Slang Translator
### **Voice-Powered Regional Heritage Explorer**
#### ALL 38 Districts | Authentic Dialects | Tamil & English

---

# 🎯 Project Overview
- **Objective:** To preserve and celebrate the rich linguistic diversity of Tamil Nadu's 38 districts.
- **Problem:** Regional slangs are often lost in translation or generalized.
- **Solution:** A dynamic, voice-enabled translator that maps authentic street slang to clear meanings.

---

# 🎙️ Key Features
- **Voice-to-Text Search:** Speak your slang in Tamil or English (using Web Speech API).
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS):** Hear the correct pronunciation of regional slangs.
- **Comprehensive Database:** 495+ authentic entries across all 38 districts.
- **District Explorer:** Interactive UI to browse regional variations from Chennai to Kanyakumari.

---

# 📍 Regional Deep Dive
- **North (Chennai):** *Madras Bashai* (e.g., Naina, Bejaar, Gethu).
- **West (Kongu):** Polite suffixes like *-nga* (e.g., Ennanga, Angutu).
- **South (Nellai/Madurai):** The signature *Le* and *Paiya* styles.
- **Delta (Thanjavur):** Traditional *Aiyya/Amma* respectful addressing.

---

# 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Modern Gradients & Animations), JavaScript.
- **Backend/Dashboard:** Python + Streamlit.
- **Data Engine:** CSV-to-JSON Pipeline for fast, scalable searching.
- **APIs:** Web Speech API for Voice Recognition and Synthesis.
